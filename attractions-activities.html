Attractions and Activities

WayFair is your ultimate guide to exploring New Zealand! Dive into the stunning landscapes, thrilling adventures, and vibrant culture that make this country a dream destination. From the iconic beauty of Milford Sound to the adrenaline rush of skydiving over Franz Josef Glacier, we've handpicked the top attractions and activities to make your trip unforgettable. Discover the magic of Hobbiton, explore geothermal wonders in Rotorua, and immerse yourself in Maori culture. Keep your eyes peeled for Great offers on some sports tickets below too! Get ready for the adventure of a lifetime in the Land of the Long White Cloud!

Milford Sound - Fiordland National Park, South Island

Experience the breathtaking beauty of Milford Sound, a stunning fiord located in Fiordland National Park. Cruise along the crystal-clear waters surrounded by towering cliffs, cascading waterfalls, and lush rainforest for an unforgettable adventure.

View More
Tongariro Alpine Crossing - Tongariro National Park, North Island

Picture yourself on one of the world's most scenic hikes, the Tongariro Alpine Crossing. This challenging yet rewarding trek takes you through dramatic volcanic landscapes, including steaming vents, emerald lakes, and stunning vistas of Mount Ruapehu.

View More
Bay of Islands - Northland, North Island

Discover the pristine beauty of the Bay of Islands, a subtropical paradise known for its clear waters, secluded beaches, and abundant marine life. Explore the area by boat, kayak, or paddleboard, and visit historic sites such as the Waitangi Treaty Grounds.

View More
Hobbiton Movie Set - Matamata, Waikato

Step into the magical world of Middle-earth with a visit to the Hobbiton Movie Set. Explore the charming hobbit holes, lush gardens, and iconic landmarks featured in the "Lord of the Rings" and "The Hobbit" film trilogies for an enchanting experience.

View More
Skydive Franz Josef Glacier - Franz Josef Glacier, West Coast

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush with a tandem skydive over Franz Josef Glacier. Soar high above the stunning glacier landscapes and snow-capped mountains as you freefall from an exhilarating altitude for an unforgettable adventure.

View More
Waitomo Glowworm Caves - Waitomo, Waikato

Journey into the mesmerizing world of the Waitomo Glowworm Caves, where thousands of bioluminescent glowworms illuminate the underground caverns. Take a boat ride through the darkened passages and marvel at the enchanting glowworm display overhead.

View More
Rotorua Geothermal Parks - Rotorua, Bay of Plenty

The geothermal wonders of Rotorua, where bubbling mud pools, steaming geysers, and colorful thermal pools showcase the Earth's natural forces. Explore parks such as Te Puia, Wai-O-Tapu, and Whakarewarewa for an immersive cultural and geothermal experience.

View More
Rugby Matches - Various Locations

Soak up New Zealand's sporting culture by attending a thrilling rugby match. Catch a game featuring the legendary All Blacks, New Zealand's national rugby team, and experience the electrifying atmosphere of a live sporting event.

FAQ’S

Accessibility: Are attractions accessible for wheelchair users?

Some attractions offer accessibility options, but it varies.

When is the best time to visit New Zealand for outdoor activities?

Summer (December to February) offers the best weather for outdoor adventures.

Beginner-Friendly Activities: Are there activities suitable for beginners?

Yes, but some activities are more challenging and may require experience or guided tours.

Health Risks: Are there any health risks associated with outdoor activities?

Yes, be cautious of weather conditions, wildlife encounters, and terrain.

Ticket Purchase: How can I purchase tickets for attractions?

Tickets can usually be bought online or at the venue, depending on availability.

Weather Impact: Can weather affect activities?

Yes, weather conditions may impact outdoor activities, so it's important to check forecasts before planning.
