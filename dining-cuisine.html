FAQ’S

What type of cuisine is popular in New Zealand?

New Zealand cuisine is diverse, with influences from Maori, European, Asian, and Pacific Island cultures. Some popular dishes include seafood, lamb, and traditional Maori hangi (a method of cooking food in an earth oven).

Are vegetarian and vegan options available at restaurants in New Zealand?

Yes, many restaurants in New Zealand offer vegetarian and vegan options. You can find a variety of plant-based dishes featuring fresh local produce.

Can I make reservations at restaurants in advance?

Yes, it's recommended to make reservations in advance, especially at popular restaurants or during peak times. You can usually make reservations online or by calling the restaurant directly.

What is the tipping etiquette in New Zealand restaurants?

Tipping is not a common practice in New Zealand, as service charges are typically included in the bill. However, if you receive exceptional service, it's appreciated to leave a small tip or round up the bill.
